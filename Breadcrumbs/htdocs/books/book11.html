Title: Ananda Math

Author: Bankim Chandra Chatterjee

Max borrow duration: 10 days

About:
Anandamath is a Bengali fiction, written by Bankim Chandra Chattopadhyay and published in 1882. It is inspired by and set in the background of the Sannyasi Rebellion in the late 18th century, it is considered one of the most important novels in the history of Bengali and Indian literature.
